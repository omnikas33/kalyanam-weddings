Welcome

Welcome to Kalyanam!

Hi! I’m Mayuri Heda, the founder and a woman whom Weddings have always seemed like magic. Maybe it’s because I’ve always been obsessed with dreamy destinations, mountains, beaches, cruises, basically anything that looks straight out of a Bollywood movie. So of course, I assumed the ideal wedding had to be all about the ideal place. But then I started thinking—why settle for just the location when there's an entire circus of planning to be done?

And that's how Kalyanam came about. I wanted a platform where couples could get everything under one roof—wedding stylists, invitations, gifts, décor and without the rabbit hole of planning stress and the headache of a million Google searches and WhatsApp forwards. With a single click, you have access to the best vendors, all personally selected so you don't have to waste time on those “mausa ji ke dost ka beta” vendor recommendations.

We have Full Wedding Packages, where we deal with everything from "Shaadi fix ho gayi!" right down to the last ‘tearful vidai’. And if you don't need the whole thing, then our Partial Packages allow you to choose the services you really do require, and we'll match you up with the best around.

And with Kalyanam, I just want to ensure that couples can experience all craziness that without losing their minds. Weddings are about loving each other, not stressing over what shade of pink your table napkins should be. We may not be able to bust out into a dance like Bittu and Shruti from Band Baaja Baaraat, but we guarantee that every wedding we organize will be as seamless as a moment in the film.

- Mayuri Heda
(Founder - Kalyanam)
